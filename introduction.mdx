## Welcome to Bridge Wrapped

Bridge Wrapped is a SaaS analytics platform that visualizes your cross-chain bridging activity from 2025. By aggregating data from Across, Relay, and LiFi protocols, you get a comprehensive view of your bridging history presented in an engaging, Spotify Wrapped-style experience.

## What you'll discover

Bridge Wrapped analyzes your 2025 bridging activity to show you:

- **Total bridging actions** across all protocols
- **Total volume** in USD for all your bridges
- **Most used chains** for both source and destination
- **Top bridged tokens** and their volumes
- **Busiest bridging day** with destination breakdown
- **Monthly activity trends** visualized in charts
- **Provider breakdown** showing your usage across Across, Relay, and LiFi
- **User class** based on transaction count and volume

<Note>
  Bridge Wrapped currently supports bridging activity from the year 2025. Historical data from previous years is not included.
</Note>

## How it works

Bridge Wrapped fetches your bridging transactions from three major protocols:

1. **Across Protocol** - Fast, secure cross-chain bridge
2. **Relay** - Multi-chain bridging and swapping
3. **LiFi** - Cross-chain liquidity aggregation

Transactions are normalized, deduplicated, and aggregated to provide accurate statistics. The platform uses RainbowKit for wallet connections and supports ENS name resolution.

<Info>
  All data is fetched in real-time from bridge protocol APIs. Bridge Wrapped does not store your transaction history.
</Info>
